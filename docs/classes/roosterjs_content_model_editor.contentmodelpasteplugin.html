Class ContentModelPastePlugin

Paste plugin, handles BeforePaste event and reformat some special content, including:

  1. Content copied from Word
  2. Content copied from Excel
  3. Content copied from Word Online or OneNote Online
  4. Content copied from Power Point (This class is still under development, and may still be changed in the future with some breaking changes)
